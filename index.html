Open @ Last
I still remember 7th November 2023 — the first time we spoke through texts.
I don’t remember the exact words I typed, or how the conversation even began…but I remember how, from that very moment, something quietly started changing inside me.
Simple messages slowly turned into memories I now hold close to my heart ✨.
On 24th April 2024, I asked you for a snap for the first time 📸.
I never knew that a small request would become such a beautiful habit.
I’ve asked you for more than a hundred snaps since then — and every single time you sent one, my heart smiled.
Seeing Myy luzzz face always made my day feel lighter. 🥹✨💖
10th February 2025 — is a day I’ll always remember with a heavy heart.
I told you about seeing my old school crush, and you teased me playfully 😅 but I took it the wrong way… my mood fell, my heart closed, and I went silent.
What I’ll never forget is you calling me again and again 📞🥺
Even when you were at home… even when your parents were there… you still tried.
I didn’t pick up even once — yet your effort that day made me realize how much I mattered to you 🤍
That meant more to me than you’ll ever know.
14th February 2025 — your birthday 🎂🌸
The first birthday of yours that I celebrated with you, Sev, and your Bhava.
That day lives rent-free in my heart 🥹 The time we spent, the photo we took 📷,and that smile on your face — That smile is still one of my favorite memories.
I swear, I’ve never forgotten it for even a second 💕
On 24th February 2025, our first PU college function 🎉.
I still remember how I said I wouldn’t come… and the moment you saw me at college, the way your expression changed — that reaction meant more to me than anything else in that entire function 🥹❤️.
24th September 2025 — I asked you to make a heart shape with your hair and send me a video 💕.
That day, I was travelling home from college, tired and drained, sitting in a bus.
The moment I saw that video, all my tiredness disappeared.
I smiled like an idiot and didn’t even know how to react 🚌💖.
26th October 2025 — after my cousin’s wedding 👰, and we didn’t talk properly for two days.
I was busy, exhausted, surrounded by people… That night when I messaged you, you were angry and said, “Don’t talk to me.”
When I asked what I should do, you told me to do 100 buski and send a video immediately, or else you wouldn’t talk 😭
Even though I was with my cousins, I tried my best, did it, sent the video… Seeing you happy after that… it made me realise every second worth it 💖
I still remember that moment clearly 🤍
On 29th October 2025, you sent me a Bestestttfriend reel and asked if we should do it together 😂💞.
We still haven’t done it… and there are two such reels waiting for us, patiently, just like some moments do 🤣✨.
And then… 16th November 2025, a random Sunday that became unforgettable 🎶🥹.
You sent me Paravashanadenu in your own voice — after I had asked you for so long.
From that very day, your voice became my ringtone… so every call could begin with you 💖.
I still smile when I think about how I used to scold you for academics — not out of anger, but care 🥺
I took ma’am’s help, collected question papers before exam, and even asked her to give you marks… and you just said, “Leave it, I know.”
That was so you 🤍

Those PU College breaks standing near the bio block during break time, just to see you, our groups teasing us while we spoke in stolen moments 🤭

And those bus rides—me waiting to go in the same bus, travelling together, yet never talking 🤣 Silence, smiles, and feelings we never said out loud.

And here comes the, year after your 18th bday
14th February 2026 🤍

Your birthday.

That day held my last quiet hope in you.
I had told myself that if you didn’t reply properly that day, I would never ask you anything again… and I would quietly stop trying to talk.

For that website, I even left my Maths semester exam and spent that time working on it.
Maybe it sounds small to others, but to me it meant a lot.
And when I saw you happy that day… it made everything feel worth it 🤍

I don’t know if you noticed it, but I was really happy because of the few words you said.
Sometimes, all I needed during my tough days was just a little of your time… nothing more.

And those caring messages you sent… they meant more to me than you know.
Did I hurt you that much somewhere?
Because honestly, you never hurt me like that.
If something went wrong, I only wish you had told me the reason before walking away.

But… it’s okay.
Maybe some things are just meant to stay as memories.

Still, I hope your day was filled with smiles.

Happy Birthday 🤍🎂✨
21st February 2026…
The first memory after your 18th birthday that still makes my heart feel full.

I told you I would give you a gift.
And that day, Nitesh and I searched almost the entire KR Puram just to find something worthy of you 🤣
Shop after shop… nothing felt right. So we decided to go all the way to Indiranagar.
We kept searching until 7 in the evening, tired but still not ready to give up.

I’ve never done that much for anyone before.
And somewhere between those streets and stores, I realised something… It wasn’t about the gift. It was about you. 🤍 And yes… I also realised shopping for a girl’s gift is a whole different level of difficulty 😮‍💨
But for you, it didn’t feel like a task. It felt special.
And then… 24th and 25th night.

Those two nights were different.
We spoke more than we ever had before.
Long conversations. Honest words. No pretending.

I opened up to you completely — my thoughts, my feelings, my fears.
And you stayed. We kept sending reels, texting, laughing, being silly for each other till almost 12 at night 🥹
I was so happy… genuinely, peacefully happy.

And that snap at 12:09 AM on 26th 🤣🤍
I still remember the exact feeling in my chest when I saw it.
28st February 2026…
The first time you mentioned me in your story after I reminded you to tag me 🤍
It was just your picture… but it meant more than you know ✨
1st March 2026 🤍

That night, after midnight, we were texting like we usually do.
Somewhere in the conversation, I asked you when I should give your gift.

You first said morning, but I was still in the village, so I said it wouldn’t be possible.
Then you casually said, “Okay, next week I’ll tell you.”

But the next morning, I kept thinking about it.
It already felt late… and waiting another week didn’t feel right to me.
So I told you I would come and give it that day itself.

You simply said, “Okay, come and give it near my house.” 🤍

I was honestly nervous. I kept thinking, what if someone sees? what if your mom is there?
But you calmly said, “It’s okay… even my mom will be there, just come and give.”

When I finally came there, one grandma nearby noticed and said, “Ohhh… see, a parcel has come.” 😅
I quietly asked you who that was and whether your mom was around.
You said, “She’s inside.”

At that moment, I didn’t have the courage to face your mom.
My heart was beating fast… So I just gave you the gift and quickly left for home.

Later, I messaged you saying that our photo was inside the gift.
You told me your mom had opened it and seen it too… and you even told her that I was the one who gave it.

And then you said something that stayed with me — that your mom liked the gift even more than you did 🤍🎁

That small moment may have looked ordinary to anyone else… but for me, it felt special.
The nervous walk, the quick meeting, the way my heart was racing —
all of it became a memory I quietly keep close to my heart. ✨
2nd March 2026 🌙

I still don’t know what exactly happened that day.

Around 12 AM, we started talking.
You had texted me earlier, but I was asleep.
When I woke up and saw your message, I felt angry at myself for missing it 🥺

After a few minutes, you replied.
Then you said you were feeling sleepy and that we would talk only for 15 minutes.

From morning, I had been waiting to talk to you.
Even though I was sleepy, I woke up just to speak with you 🤍
But when you said that, something inside me felt hurt.

You told me,
“If you’re sleepy, then sleep. Who told you not to? If I’m awake, I’ll text. Before sleeping, I’ll say good night.”

But I wasn’t worried about sleep.
I just wanted to talk… properly.
I was waiting to share things with you.
After that, my mood went off and I became silent.

Then you said,
“Why should we talk every day? Just because we talk daily doesn’t mean we are friends. Once I consider someone a friend, it’s not compulsory to talk daily.”
You also said you don’t expect anything from anyone.

I said sorry for everything.
But you said, “Don’t overact now.”

At the end, I just want you to understand one thing, maa 🤍
I only wanted to talk to you every day — with or without a topic.
Because I feel comfortable with you. That’s it.

It’s been three years… and sometimes I wonder, is this all you understood about me?
I changed my perspective many times just to keep you in my life.
At least for my efforts, show me a little understanding.

Do you think any boy would leave his ego and self-respect and still stay like this?
After everything, when I feel treated like I don’t matter, it hurts, maa. That’s all 💔

I know you have treated me as a priority many times.
You have made me smile, done things I asked for, and cared for me 🤍
I’m not denying that. But sometimes, small behaviours hurt more than big fights.
You know how much I overthink…

You texted me many times in the morning. But I controlled myself.
I was hurting myself by not texting you back.

That day stayed with me. 🤍✨
No proper conversation between us after that😖💔
That night, I got a request from an Instagram account with your name. I knew it wasn’t you—it felt like a fake account. Still, I messaged it just to find out who it was. By then, you had already come to know about it and even called me, but I didn’t answer. You told me not to text that account, saying it was fake.

But I really wanted to know who it was and why they were doing all this… especially about us 🥺 You told me to just take screenshots of whatever they sent. At one point, they asked about our relationship. I sent you that screenshot, and you told me to reply that we were just friends, not lovers.

The next morning, the account got deactivated… and I thought everything was solved 🤍
Again, I got another request with your name. This time, I thought it was really you since your main account was deactivated, so I texted normally. Later, I realised it was fake again. This time, I really wanted to know who that person was.
You came to know that I was texting that account and got upset. You said I should have texted you on another account, or on WhatsApp or Snapchat. But I had already tried messaging you before that, and you didn’t reply… so I continued 🥺
You told me to leave you alone. But at that time, you already knew who it was and why they were doing this, I think. I tried my best to make you understand and just asked you to tell me who it was and why… but you didn’t want to explain at all.

At one point, I even started doubting myself—is all this because of me? You said no… but still told me to leave you alone.

When I asked if it was irritating that I was trying to understand your problem, you said yes… And when I asked if this is what friendship is, you still said yes.

That hurt me more than anything 💔 Even after that, all I was worried about was your exam… nothing more.
But at that moment, it felt like maybe I didn’t mean anything to you at all.
Even though we weren’t talking, I still wished you for your exam and told you all the best. When you replied saying there was no misunderstanding between us, it made me really happy 🥺
I felt like maybe things were okay again… You had said earlier that you would tell me how your exam went, so I waited the whole day… just hoping for one message.

At least one reply… even to my old messages… But there was nothing.

You had time to post a snap at 7 in the morning and update your status… you even went to Wonderla. But there was no time to reply to me. Maybe this feels childish to you…
But when I say you are important to me, I truly mean it. You are my priority 🤍

And in my bucket list, I had even mentioned clearly—before going out, just send me a snap… But not even that happened.

I’m not saying you don’t prioritize me… I’m saying you do it only when you feel like it or when you have time. But most of the time, when I need you the most… you are not there.

That’s what hurts me the most 🥺
I’m honestly tired of feeling like this. If you don’t want me in your life, just tell me directly… I’ll accept it.

It won’t be easy for me… but I’ll try. Maybe it’s just my fate…
But still… somewhere, I’m holding onto a little hope 🤍✨
The promises we made… to spend more time together in the future, to take pictures, make reels, do video calls… Those weren’t just words to me. I kept them safely in my heart.

I’m waiting for your exams to get over, maa.
Waiting for the day we can sit and talk without checking the time.
Waiting to make those promises real, luzzzzzz 🤍✨

These memories may look small to the world.
But to me… they were moments where my heart felt closest to yours.

— always, with feelings I never fully learned how to hide 🌙💫

These aren’t just dates or events. They are pieces of my heart, scattered across time — all connected to you 🤍.

And before I end this letter…

Now you’ve chosen the harder path—taking a drop to chase your dream 🩺✨
I truly believe in you, maa. You will achieve your goal 🤍

Always yours 🫶,
— nayiiiiiiiiiii🐶
